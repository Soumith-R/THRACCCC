About Us

THRCCCC is a non-government, non-profit organisation registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882. We are connected with NITI Aayog. We work every day to protect people's rights, stop corruption, and control crime so that Telangana can be a safe, fair, and peaceful place for everyone.
