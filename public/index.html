About THRCCCC

The Telangana Human Rights Anti-Corruption and Crime Control Council (THRACCCC) is a legally registered non-profit organization formed under the provisions of the Indian Trusts Act, 1882. The organization is recognized and certified by NITI Aayog, MSME (Udyam Registration), DARPAN (NGO Partnership System, NITI Aayog), and is also an ISO 9001:2015 certified body, ensuring quality standards in governance and operations.

The Council actively engages in awareness programs, advocacy, and legal support to empower youth, women, and marginalized communities, while working in collaboration with government authorities, law enforcement, and civil society. With a foundation rooted in legality, recognition through national certifications, and commitment to constitutional values, THRACCCC stands as a credible and transparent institution dedicated to building a corruption-free, just, and rights-protected Telangana.
