Human rights

Every person has inherent dignity and value. One of the ways we honour this is by acknowledging and respecting human rights. Human rights are a set of principles based on equality and fairness. They protect our freedom to make choices about our lives, develop our potential, and live free from fear, harassment, or discrimination.

Human rights can be described as a collection of basic rights and freedoms that people around the world agree are essential for a dignified life. These include the right to life, the right to a fair trial, freedom from torture or cruel treatment, freedom of speech, freedom of religion, and the rights to health, education, and a decent standard of living.

These rights apply to everyone, everywhere – men and women, young and old, rich and poor – regardless of background, beliefs, or place of residence. This is why human rights are called universal.

To live with dignity, all people must enjoy certain freedoms and protections. Human rights safeguard individuals from abuses that undermine their dignity and ensure they have the opportunities needed to reach their full potential, free from discrimination.

Human rights include civil, political, economic, social, cultural, and environmental rights, such as:

  • The right to freedom of expression
  • The right to freedom of religion or conscience
  • The right to property
  • The right to peaceful assembly
  • The right to privacy
  • The right to vote
  • The right to access clean water and a healthy environment
  • The right to work and receive fair wages
  • The right to social security
  • The right to participate in cultural life
  • The right to be free from slavery and forced labour
  • The right to personal security and protection from violence

About Us

THRCCCC is a non-government, non-profit organisation registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882. We are connected with NITI Aayog. We work every day to protect people's rights, stop corruption, and control crime so that Telangana can be a safe, fair, and peaceful place for everyone.
