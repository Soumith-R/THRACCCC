Our True-selves

Organization

The Telangana Human Rights Anti-Corruption and Crime Control Council (THRCCCC) was started in August 2025 to work for the protection of human rights, to fight against corruption, and to reduce crime in society. Our aim is to make sure every person is treated with respect and fairness, to promote peace and harmony, and to create a transparent system free from corruption. We also focus on spreading awareness, giving education on rights, and encouraging friendship and cooperation between people at the state, national, and international levels.

THRCCCC is a non-government, non-profit organisation registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882. We are connected with NITI Aayog. We work every day to protect people's rights, stop corruption, and control crime so that Telangana can be a safe, fair, and peaceful place for everyone.

Securing Human Rights under

  • Human Rights Protection Act, 1993 – We follow and promote this law to safeguard the rights of every citizen.
  • Universal Declaration of Human Rights (1948) – Adopted by the United Nations, we support and work towards its principles.
  • Protect human rights at both individual and community levels.
  • Safeguard women's rights with a strong focus on women's safety, equality, and protection from all forms of violence.
  • Ensure the protection of children's rights and promote education for all.
  • Promote environmental protection as a human right, ensuring clean air, water, and a healthy ecosystem for all.
  • Empower youth by creating opportunities for leadership, skill development, and active participation in nation-building, while addressing the challenges of drug abuse and narcotics through awareness, prevention, and rehabilitation programs.
  • Support and develop rehabilitation centres for vulnerable groups, victims of abuse, those affected by crime or addiction, and youth involved in drugs or narcotics.
  • Provide care, protection, and education for orphans, ensuring their rights and future security.
  • Prevent criminal activities across the country.
  • Assist administrative bodies in maintaining peace, harmony, and unity among communities.

About Us

National Human Rights and Crime Control Bureau (NHRCCB) is functioning with commitment to the Noble Cause of Human Rights Protection and Promotion Justice, Education, Love, Peace, Harmony to all and Friendship, National & International Integration by Exchange of Ideas & Ethos around the Nation.
